Day 1: TRIP TO LUXOR

I've been to Luxor a number of times, and I think it's definitely the most awe-inspiring and unforgettable location I've ever been. This might be the best location to take pictures in as well! The amount of historical sites available is plenty! I'll definitely visit again.I went to see the Kings' Valley and Queen Hatshepsut's tomb. The sights were beautiful and our tour guide was also very friendly and informative! I had a truly fabulous time. At the last minute, I wanted to visit the Luxor Museum and I'm happy that I did! When in Luxor, don't miss it.
